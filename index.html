Bio

Ferruccio Lamborghini was an Italian entrepreneur who founded Lamborghini, a luxury sports car brand, in 1963. Prior to entering the automotive industry, he made his fortune as a manufacturer of tractors and air conditioning units. Lamborghini's passion for cars led him to create his own line of high-performance vehicles
